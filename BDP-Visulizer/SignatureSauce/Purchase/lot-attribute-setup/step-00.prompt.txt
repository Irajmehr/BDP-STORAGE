TOPIC: "Lot Attribute Setup" — Step 0: Overview

WHAT THIS STEP COVERS:
The Lot Attribute Setup page defines how lot attributes are structured across your entire system. This extension creates a predefined matrix of valid lot configurations per item, enabling precise traceability, quality grading, and compliance tracking for every lot that enters your warehouse.

DETAILED CONTEXT:
Food distributors need lot attributes to answer critical questions: Where did this product come from? What grade is it? When does it expire? Lot attributes make recalls surgical instead of catastrophic, keep you FDA-compliant, and let you price products based on actual quality received.

KEY NARRATION POINTS:
Lot attributes are the backbone of traceability in a food distribution operation. Every lot of product that enters your warehouse carries characteristics — where it was grown, what grade it received, when it expires, who supplied it. The Lot Attribute Setup page in this extension defines the structure for capturing all of that information consistently across every item and every transaction. Think of it as building a blueprint: you decide which attributes matter for your business, and the system enforces that blueprint every time a lot is created. For a seafood distributor handling FDA-regulated imports, this means country of origin is never missing.
