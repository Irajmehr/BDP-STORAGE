WHAT THIS STEP COVERS:
Auto Assign CASE Nos. in Inbound Entries automatically generates CASE ledger entry numbers during warehouse receiving. Allow Manual CASE Nos. lets users override the auto-assigned number when needed.

DETAILED CONTEXT:
For high-volume receiving — 200+ lines per day — auto-assign saves roughly 30 seconds per line. Manual override is useful when you need to match a supplier CASE number for traceability. Most operations leave auto-assign ON and manual override ON as a safety valve.

KEY NARRATION POINTS:
When your receiving team is processing a truck with 200 lines of product, every second counts. in Inbound Entries tells the system to automatically generate CASE ledger entry numbers during warehouse receiving, so your team does not have to manually type or select a number for each case entry. That saves roughly 30 seconds per line — which adds up to over an hour on a large delivery. The Allow Manual CASE Nos. toggle works alongside auto-assign as a safety valve.
