TOPIC: "Alt Uom Setup" — Step 3: Document Visibility

WHAT THIS STEP COVERS:
Show Alt UOM Additional Fields on PO/SO controls whether CASE columns appear on purchase order and sales order lines. When OFF, users only see base UOM columns and must order in KG or LB.

DETAILED CONTEXT:
If this toggle is OFF, your purchasing team will not see CASE Qty, CASE Size, or CASE Weight columns on PO lines. They will be forced to order in base UOM only. Turn it ON so buyers can enter "50 cases" instead of calculating "500 KG" manually.

KEY NARRATION POINTS:
Here is a setting that trips up new implementations: Show Alt UOM Additional Fields on PO and SO. If this toggle is OFF, your purchase order lines and sales order lines will only show base UOM columns — Quantity, Unit of Measure, Unit Cost. Your purchasing team will be forced to calculate how many kilograms they need and enter that number manually. Turn it ON, and suddenly they see CASE Qty, CASE Size, and CASE Weight columns right on the document line. A buyer can type "50" in the CASE Qty field and the system fills in the rest.
