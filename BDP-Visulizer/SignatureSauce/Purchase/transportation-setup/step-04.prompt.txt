TOPIC: "Transportation Setup" — Step 4: TO Templates — Reusable Charge Sets

WHAT THIS STEP COVERS:
TO Templates bundle default charges with estimated amounts and assign them to vendors or shipment methods. When a PO is created for a vendor with a template, the estimated charges flow in automatically. Templates eliminate manual charge entry and ensure consistency.

DETAILED CONTEXT:
Your Norway seafood supplier always has Freight + Customs + Insurance. Create a template with these 3 charges and estimated amounts. Every new PO auto-gets the right charges — no one has to remember which costs apply to which vendor.

KEY NARRATION POINTS:
TO Templates eliminate the manual work of entering charges on every Transportation Order. A template is a reusable set of charges with estimated amounts, assigned to a specific vendor or shipment method. Take your Norway seafood supplier: every shipment from them involves ocean freight, customs clearance, and marine insurance. You create a template with those three charges and plug in estimated amounts based on historical averages — say $2,500 for freight, $800 for customs, and $350 for insurance. Now every time a buyer creates a Purchase Order for that vendor, the system automatically pulls in those three charges with the estimated amounts.
