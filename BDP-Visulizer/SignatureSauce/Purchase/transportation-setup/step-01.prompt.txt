WHAT THIS STEP COVERS:
The TMS Setup page is the global configuration hub. It defines number series for Transportation Orders and Posted Transportation Orders, enables Freight Zones, and sets Charge Amount Rounding Precision. Every TMS operation reads from this page.

TPO Nos. and Posted TPO Nos. are mandatory — without them, the system cannot create a single Transportation Order. Freight Zones Used enables geographic charge grouping. Charge Amount Rounding Precision controls decimal accuracy on allocated costs.
