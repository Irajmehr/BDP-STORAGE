WHAT THIS STEP COVERS:
Inecta Food allows users to manage decisions related to Purchase Order/Invoice processes. Specific setups determine how the system behaves during purchases and apply to all purchase documents for all users.

DETAILED CONTEXT:
These settings are configured in Purchases & Payables Setup, accessed via Tell Me > Purchases & Payables Setup.

The Purchases and Payables Setup page is where your entire purchasing process comes together. Before your team processes a single Purchase Order, you need to configure how the system behaves — pricing rules, approval gates, cost tolerances, lot management, and document controls. These settings live in the Purchases and Payables Setup page, and they apply to all purchase documents for all users. Getting them right upfront saves your team from errors, disputes, and compliance headaches down the road.
