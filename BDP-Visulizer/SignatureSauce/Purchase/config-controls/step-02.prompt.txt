TOPIC: "Config Controls" — Step 2: Approval & Stage Controls

WHAT THIS STEP COVERS:
PO Stage Status tracks where unposted Purchase Orders are (e.g., Waiting for Approval, Ready for Resourcing, Quote Sent). PO Version/Revision No. tracks changes to PO Lines. Approval workflows route to correct approver.

DETAILED CONTEXT:
PO Stage Status Changed By and Change Date/Time fields provide audit trail. Stage statuses are user-defined — no defaults provided. Viewable and filterable on the PO List.

KEY NARRATION POINTS:
Now let's look at approval and stage controls. The PO Stage Status feature lets you define custom stages that track where each unposted Purchase Order sits in your workflow. For our cheese distributor, you might create stages like "Waiting for Accounting Approval," "Pending Supplier Confirmation," and "Ready to Release." Each time a user changes the stage, the system records who made the change and the exact date and time, giving you a complete audit trail. These stages are visible and filterable on the Purchase Order List, so your procurement manager can quickly see which orders are stuck and where. PO Version and Revision Number tracking adds another layer — every time a PO line is modified, the revision increments, so you always know exactly what changed between the original order and what was finally received.
