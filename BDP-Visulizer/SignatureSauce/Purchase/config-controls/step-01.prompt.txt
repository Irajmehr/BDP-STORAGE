Last Direct Cost from Item Card flows to PO lines by default. Toggle "Do Not Flow Last Direct Cost" to require manual entry. BUOM Cost toggle controls whether user enters cost per UOM or per Base UOM.

KEY NARRATION POINTS:
First up: pricing controls. Inecta uses a priority hierarchy for purchase pricing. At the top, you have Vendor-specific Purchase Prices by Item — these are negotiated rates for specific cheeses from specific suppliers. Next, Purchase Line Discounts kick in based on quantity thresholds, so ordering a full pallet of aged cheddar might earn a five percent discount. Then there is Purchase Price by Lot Attributes, which allows different pricing for the same cheese based on characteristics like origin, aging period, or organic certification.
