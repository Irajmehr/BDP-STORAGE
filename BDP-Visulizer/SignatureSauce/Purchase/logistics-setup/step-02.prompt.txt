Logistics Orders progress through a defined lifecycle: Shipped, Received, Delivered, Invoiced. Status changes happen automatically based on registered and posted warehouse activities, keeping every stakeholder informed in real time.

DETAILED CONTEXT:
When your warehouse posts the receipt, the Logistics Order automatically moves from Shipped to Received. Your operations team sees the status update in real time without manual intervention.

One of the most powerful aspects of Logistics Orders is the automatic status lifecycle. Every order moves through four stages: Shipped, Received, Delivered, and Invoiced. Here is what makes this valuable — the status changes are driven by actual warehouse activities, not manual updates. When your warehouse team posts the receipt for that salmon container, the Logistics Order automatically moves from Shipped to Received.
