For ocean shipments like your frozen shrimp container from Vietnam, Inecta supports EDI 301 and EDI 315 document tracking directly on the Transportation Order. EDI 301 captures booking details: the Shipping Agent SCAC Code, Bill of Lading number, Vessel Name, Voyage Number, and Booking Issue Date. As the container moves, EDI 315 updates the TO with event dates, actual shipment and receipt dates, and container details including Container ID, seal numbers, and prefix codes. This means your logistics team can track exactly where that container of shrimp is at any point in the journey, from the port of Ho Chi Minh City to your cold storage dock.
